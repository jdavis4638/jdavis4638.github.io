About us

Are you tired of the daily grind, the 9-to-5 routine that feels more like a stale crouton than a fulfilling career? Well, we've got the dough for you! Welcome to "Rise & Shine Sourdough," where we take bread to new heights, and we're looking for a few passionate loaf lovers to join our bubbly, doughy team.

At Rise & Shine Sourdough, we believe in the power of sourdough magic. We're not just a bakery; we're a family of fermenters, a society of starters, and a club of culture carriers. When you join us, you're not just getting a job; you're becoming a bona fide bread wizard.

Who we're looking for:

As a Dough Maestro, your daily rituals will include:
  • breakfast_diningCrafting artisanal sourdough bread that's fluffier than a cloud.
  • breakfast_diningNurturing our sourdough starters like they're your own offspring.
  • breakfast_diningAdding a dash of creativity to every batch, whether it's a classic boule, a twisted pretzel, or an experimental sourdough croissant.
  • breakfast_diningSpreading the word about the wonders of wild fermentation. No, you're not a salesperson; you're a sourdough evangelist!

What's in Your Starter Kit?

  • A top-notch apron, because a Dough Maestro deserves the best.

    This apron is not just any apron; it's a badge of honor for sourdough artisans. Made from organic flour bags (just kidding), it's stylish, durable, and guaranteed to make you look like a pro in the kitchen.

  • Sourdough starter adoption privileges.

    Adopt your very own sourdough starter! We'll match you with a starter that suits your personality. Will it be Mild Mabel, Bold Bob, or Quirky Quinn? Start your sourdough journey with your new sour friend.

  • Unlimited access to our secret library of bread puns.

    We have a treasure trove of bread puns that will make you laugh, cringe, and appreciate the doughy side of life. Puns are like bread, you can never have enough.

  • A warm, doughy welcome from our quirky crew.

    Our team is passionate about all things sourdough, and we're here to welcome you into our dough-loving family. Expect warm greetings, doughy hugs, and plenty of floury handshakes.

  • The chance to shape your future. Literally!

    With our starter kit, you get the opportunity to shape more than just dough. You'll shape your own destiny, one delicious loaf at a time. The power of sourdough is in your hands!
